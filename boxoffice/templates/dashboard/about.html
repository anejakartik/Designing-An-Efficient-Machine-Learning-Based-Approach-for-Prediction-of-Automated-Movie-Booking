About Us

Our Journey

BoxOffice focus on developing captivating architectural designs make the multiplexes look aspirational and also complete the ‘Experience’ promise. Driven by its passion to improvise unremittingly and deliver moments which may be treasured by patrons, BoxOffice is committed towards operating a sustainable, responsible and profitable business venture and attain new levels of operational brilliance.

Our Goals
